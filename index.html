FAQs

Q: Is my camper required to be vaccinated to go to camp this summer?

A: No.

Q: Where can I see my camper's camp pictures?

A: Visit Bunk1!

Q: Can my camper bring their phone to camp?

A: No. We have a zero-tolerance cell-phone policy at Camp Don Lee.

Q: Can I add money to my camper's store account in-person.

A: Adding funds to camper store accounts can only be done through your Bunk1 account.

About

Don Lee Camp & Retreat Center is affiliated by faith with The North Carolina Conference of the United Methodist Church and operated by NC United Methodist Camp & Retreat Ministries, Inc.

Don Lee is nestled on the north shore of the Neuse River near Oriental, North Carolina. Don Lee Camp & Retreat Center is an ideal setting to learn sailing, explore God’s creation and grow in faith.
